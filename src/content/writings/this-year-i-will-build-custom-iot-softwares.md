As of 12.05.2026 I started to work on my business full-time. I'm now offering
my software knowledge, skills and experience as a service. But it is not
gonna work out if I market it this way, so instead I'm building custom
IoT dashboards for industrial businesses (if you're interested, you can book
a call 😉).

Hardware companies are great at their craft, they know how to program their
firmwares, client sales, packaging etc.. but as I noticed most of them don't
deliver good quality customer dashboards or they don't have internal systems
that runs their business. Here is where I come to play, I help your business
to be analyzable and automatable.

You might ask why me? Because I built this type of solutions for over two years
and delivered production grade software to the end-user. Plus, I know hardware.
I built complex hardware solutions from CO2 sensors to GPS tracking systems that
end-to-end built by me.

My plan to get started: I'll share demo projects to showcase what is possible.
And try reaching to companies that can get great value from my services. I'll
try to share my journey as open as possible so maybe my experience can inspire
others too.

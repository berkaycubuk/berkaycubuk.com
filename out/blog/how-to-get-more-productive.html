03.02.2021
In today's world, getting distracted is super easy. Also, for a big chunk of young people, doing something is worthless. And I want to disagree with that. We're human because we can do whatever we want. We can build softwares, buildings, and new technologies. To move humanity forward, we have to do. Ok, but how to create more? Well, as I understood, you have to force yourself to create something every day. Currently, I'm writing on my blog every morning, and I feel better.
